Star Wars: Echoes of the Force


It is a time of questionable peace. Jedi Knight Jack Starwhisker, wielder of the triple-bladed glowstick saber, has been banished from the Jedi Order for excessive snack breaks and insisting that “the Force feels strongest after tacos.” Now exiled to the moon of Flavortown-9, Jack trains under the mysterious and possibly imaginary Master Broccolini, who communicates only in interpretive dance.

Meanwhile, a dark plot brews. The sinister Sith Lord Croutonus, Jack’s former roommate and part-time karaoke rival, has acquired the mythical Artifact of Awkward Silence — capable of rendering any negotiation unbearably cringey. With his trusty space llama Cheesesteak and a broken droid that only speaks in outdated memes, Jack must rise once more to stop the galaxy from plunging into galactic discomfort... one awkward moment at a time.
